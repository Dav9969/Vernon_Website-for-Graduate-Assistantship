History of HCI

Human-Interaction Computer became known in the 1980s around the same time people owned computers in their homes. Technology was enhancing each year allowing machines and electronics to become more human like. HCI examines how people interact with computer technology, software and human behavior. HCI teaches you how to create or design new methodologies and interfaces to provide a more appealing look for users.

Evolution of HCI!

From the picture above we can see the evolution of HCI and how it has changed so much over time. From punch cards to voice controlled smart speakers, HCI continues to grow rapidly that provides powerful learning experiences and improved solutions to business challenges. Computer Interaction designs effortless systems for companies that will be versatile for tech-super-users and non-tech users.
