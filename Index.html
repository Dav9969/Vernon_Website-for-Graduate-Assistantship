Life With HCI!

If you enjoy working on the computer and would like to study more about the interactions between humans, Human Computer Interaction (HCI) is the program for you! HCI is a program that specifies in computer software and designing new modifications to make the use of a computer easier to people! I have a background in Fine Arts and Fashion and Textile Design Technologies, where I studied graphic design, algebra, drawing the human figure, computer-aided design (CAD), sustainability issues and textile evaluation.

In textile evaluation, I researched textile history and examined various standard test methods of different fabrics. Textile testing prevents mislabeled fabrics before the manufacturing process. The purpose of these tests is to ensure the safety of the consumer when buying different fabrics. As fabric testing continues, more innovative clothing will provide the best possible product for people. Human-computer interaction also has similar intentions that create emerging technologies to build relationships with future consumers, honor safe products, and deliver excellent finished products.

I also studied a null and alternative statistical hypothesis. I tested different levels of significance to determine specific surveys. HCI also has the goal of concluding accurate information and results. Developing different formulas and techniques makes it easy to identify problems and allows for safer interactions for better results. My undergraduate studies has inspired me pursue to the technical details of design while still working on the computer. My website gives you a better understanding of HCI and provides details on the necessary skills that are needed.
