Current Information About HCI

We often use some of the components from HCI and we don't even know it! Let's think about how computer's have grown over the past few years. Many computers were only used in schools or in offices. However, we can now use them at home or even carry them in our backpacks, better known as laptops! The design of computers have been adjusted according to the liking of users. They are now more convenient and popular in today's world. Companies often use HCI to increases more user-friendly products and also meets the needs of people with disabilites. For example, there are keyboards that have bigger letters for people that struggle with their vision. HCI also includes a voice reading feature that reads the documents out loud. If you would like to pursue HCI, below are a list of important details you should know!
